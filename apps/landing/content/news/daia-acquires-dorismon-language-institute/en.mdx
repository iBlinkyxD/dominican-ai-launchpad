# DAIA Announces Strategic Acquisition of Dorismon English Institute, Appoints Luis Dorismon as Chief Learning Officer

**Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic** - The Dominican Artificial
Intelligence Association (DAIA) is proud to announce its first strategic
acquisition: **Dorismon English Institute**, a pioneering English-language
learning institution known for its human-centered teaching and deep
community roots. The acquisition, finalized for an undisclosed amount,
marks a major milestone in DAIA's mission to shape the future of
education through the power of artificial intelligence and
purpose-driven leadership.

At the heart of this acquisition is a shared vision-_to teach one
million Dominicans English_-and a shared belief: that **language is not
just a tool, but a gateway to opportunity, purpose, and global
relevance**.

DAIA's Founder and Executive Leadership have long championed a
"people-first" philosophy in technology deployment. This acquisition was
inspired by a two-and-a-half-year collaborative relationship between
**Dorismon English Institute** and DAIA, during which **Mr. Luis Dorismon**,
founder and CEO of Dorismon English Institute, consistently
distinguished himself through **exceptional stewardship, unwavering
passion, and purpose-driven pedagogy**. Mr. Dorismon's ability to teach
English with compassion, precision, and personal investment made a
lasting impression on the DAIA leadership and community alike.

> "Prof. Dorismon didn't just teach English-he taught it as if the future of the
> learner mattered to him personally. That's the kind of leadership DAIA
> needs," said a DAIA spokesperson.

As part of the acquisition, Mr. Dorismon will be appointed **Chief
Learning Officer** of DAIA and will spearhead the development and
execution of DAIA's new **AI-Powered English Language Learning Program**, an
initiative central to the organization's national vision for accessible
education and AI literacy.

More than an educator, **Luis Dorismon is a man of deep integrity and
spiritual stewardship**, a representative of Kingdom Ambassadorship and a
devoted member of the Church of Christ. His grounding in faith and
commitment to service aligns powerfully with DAIA's values of
**excellence, responsibility, and reverence**. In building this next
chapter, DAIA is not only acquiring an institute-it is honoring and
amplifying a legacy.

> "This acquisition speaks volumes to the confidence we have in the next
> generation of Dominican leadership," said a senior DAIA executive. "We
> believe Luis Dorismon is the cornerstone of what we intend to
> build-an education system that puts God, purpose, and people at the
> center."

With this acquisition, DAIA reinforces its standing at the **forefront of
AI-integrated education in the Caribbean**, while laying a strong
foundation for its flagship goal: **to empower and equip one million
Dominicans with the English language**-unlocking career pathways,
entrepreneurship, and international collaboration at scale.

## **About DAIA**

The Dominican Artificial Intelligence Association (DAIA) is a visionary
institution committed to equipping the Dominican Republic for the future
of artificial intelligence. Through a combination of education, policy
advocacy, and strategic technology deployment, DAIA is focused on
building sovereign, community-centered AI infrastructures that
prioritize people over systems.

## **About Dorismon English Institute**

Founded by educator and leader **Luis Dorismon**, the Dorismon English
Institute has helped hundreds of students master English through a
unique blend of culturally relevant instruction, Christ-centered values,
and community empowerment. The institute has earned a reputation for
producing results not only in fluency but in character and confidence.
