the multiplier should be fixed or vary by configuration. Update running averages after each entry.

## Notes

- **Key finding (2 samples):** W/L-Min varies significantly by lesson length — shorter lessons are denser:
  - 5m lessons: Written **137.9**, Narration **142.7** (formula slightly underestimates narration at 130)
  - 15m lessons: Written **93.6**, Narration **116.2** (formula significantly overestimates written at 150)
- The formula likely needs **separate multipliers by lesson length tier**, not a single fixed value.
- Narration at 5m actually *exceeded* the 130 estimate (110% accuracy) — first time formula underestimated.
- Collect ≥20m data and more Beginner samples before updating the formula constants.
